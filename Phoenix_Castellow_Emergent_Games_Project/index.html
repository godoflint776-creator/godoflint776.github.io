About Us

Emergent Games is a small indie development studio focused on creating, emergent games. We prioritize emergent design in our experiences, we offer games with little to no non traditional tutorials (tutorials ARE present, but they are integrated through gameplay rather than dialogue or written word.), we are confident in our ability to tell our stories and messages almost solely through gameplay.
