Pixel World Adventure

Genre: Roleplaying Game

Pixel World Adventure is our first game, and is our most simple. It's sort of sanbox rpg hybrid, where the fun is teaching the game to play itself, using the AI in the game and teaching them lessons through game design that you make yourself, in an admittedly structured environment. While it uses RPG tropes, it is not a traditional JRPG, and is instead more of an experience in which you create your own fun.
